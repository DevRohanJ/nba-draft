Chemistry System

The Chemistry score is based on a combination of factors between your starting players.

Bonuses

  • Positional Fit: +10 for each player in their natural position.
  • Archetype Synergy: +4 for each link between adjacent, complementary players.
  • Same Team: +15 for each pair of starters from the same NBA team.
  • Same Conference: +5 for each pair of starters from the same conference (but not the same team).

Final Score

The raw score (sum of all bonuses) is multiplied by 0.6 and then capped at 100 to get the final Chemistry score.
